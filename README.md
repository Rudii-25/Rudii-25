 `root@phantom:~# . /dev/null`

**Architect of Digital Chaos.**
Operating in the spaces between the code. My focus is on the lifecycle of a breach—from the initial delivery of custom-crafted payloads to the darker layers of the stack. While others study how systems work, I study how they bleed.
**Core Competencies:**
*   **Remote Access:** Engineering bespoke RATs and stealthy backdoors designed for long-term persistence.
*   **Infection Vectors:** Developing sophisticated payloads that bypass EDR and live in memory Ensuring that once I’m in, I stay in.
*   **Command & Control:** Building robust C2 infrastructures to manage compromised nodes across distributed networks.
*   **Exploitation:** Stripping systems down to their weakest points and forcing them to execute my will.
Digging deep into the architecture of failure. If there is a crack in the foundation, I will find it, widen it, and crawl through.

**Status:** `Hunting for 0-days.`
